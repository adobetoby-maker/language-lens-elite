# Language Threshold — Product Marketing Context

name: Language Threshold
oneliner: Professional-grade Spanish (and 6 other languages) for medical workers, tradespeople, and families — with AI feedback built in.
url: https://app.languagethreshold.com
pricing_url: https://app.languagethreshold.com/pricing

what_it_does: |
  Language Threshold is a premium language learning app focused on professional vocabulary —
  660+ lessons across medical (390 lessons, 13 specialties) and construction/trades (270 lessons,
  9 trades). It adds AI word cards with morphology breakdown, speaking practice with AI feedback,
  grammar drills, conjugation trainer, sentence builder, daily challenges, and a leaderboard.
  A Family plan includes Junior Linguist for ages 4-12 with kids vocab, stories, and games.
  Supports 7 languages: Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese.

product_type: SaaS / language learning app
business_model: Freemium subscription — free missionary tier, $19/mo or $149/yr Pro, $249/yr Family

target_audiences:
  primary:
    - Medical workers (nurses, NPs, PAs, doctors, OR staff, hospital EVS) who need professional Spanish
    - Construction/trades workers (foreman, electrician, plumber, framer, etc.) — job-site Spanish
  secondary:
    - LDS missionaries — free Missionary Spanish module, strong word-of-mouth community
    - Families with young children — Family plan + Junior Linguist
    - General language learners — Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese

core_problem: |
  Duolingo teaches you how to ask where the bathroom is. Language Threshold teaches you
  how to explain a surgery to a patient, run a job site, or prep a missionary for the field.
  Professional workers need domain-specific vocabulary — not tourist phrases.

key_differentiators:
  - 660+ professional lessons (medical + construction) — not general/tourist vocabulary
  - AI speaking feedback — not just flashcards
  - AI word cards with morphology breakdown
  - Family plan bundles adult Pro + Junior Linguist (kids 4-12) in one subscription
  - Free missionary tier creates organic LDS community acquisition
  - 7 languages in one app
  - Premium editorial feel — not gamified/cartoon

top_competitors:
  - Duolingo (gamified, general, free — low professional vocabulary)
  - Babbel (subscription, general — no AI, no professional focus)
  - Rosetta Stone (expensive, general — no professional focus)
  - MedicalSpanish.com (narrow, one specialty, no AI)
  - Pimsleur (audio-only, no professional vocabulary)
  - SpanishPod101 (broad, video-heavy, no professional focus)

pricing:
  free: Missionary Spanish (always free, no account required)
  monthly: $19/month — 7-day free trial
  annual: $149/year ($12.42/mo) — 7-day free trial — 35% savings
  family: $249/year — everything in Pro + Junior Linguist + 6 seats

brand_voice: Premium, editorial, confident. Not gamified. Not cartoon. Not "fun." — Serious about language, approachable in delivery. Think Calm meets Duolingo's reach with a professional focus.

biggest_gap: No marketing landing page — app IS the entry point. No SEO surface. No ad landing pages.
